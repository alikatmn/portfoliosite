Playplan.com

Playplan.com is a website where caregivers of children of all ages can come to create their daily or weekly ‘plan’. The site features daily-updated local activities, events, and ideas which will keep kids learning, active and entertained... making the caregivers job easier and more fulfilling.

Business Concept

Parenting is hard. PlayPlan combines two things can make it easier, play and a plan. Playplan.com has become an invaluable resource for caregivers, a place they visit daily to find the newest, coolest LOCAL things to do with their kids. Caregivers depend on Playplan.com to provide them with the tools needed to give their kids every opportunity to learn, laugh and grow by gathering all of the local events, storytimes, parks, and activities into one easily accessible site.

Logo Design

At PlayPlan.com, we find fun. The PlayPlan.com logo was designed to convey that idea with the custom online map marker icon that also gives the feeling of a child's spinning toy top.

Website Design

Take a look at most sites that are built for caregivers of young children and you will find a mishmash of bright colors, child-like scripted typefaces, and a startling amount of rainbows and butterflies. While designing Playplan.com, we knew it was important that this website be built for the caregiver, not the child. The site was designed to be a welcoming, organized space that conveys the playful and creative spirit of the children, without becoming a sample of the childrens artwork.

Website Development

The two most important aspects of Playplan.com is to make it easily updated and customized. It was developed to pull many event descriptions, times and places from multiple websites, and has been built to be easily customizable for local franchisees.

Application Design and Development

Although Playplan.com was designed as a responsive website, future design and development of a stand-alone mobil application is in process.
